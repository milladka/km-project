"Fourth nature" (4th NATURE) is a theoretical concept of larger natural units arranged by man, which show high complexity through the connection of nature and technology, infrastructure and landscape. The name "4th NATURE" itself is a play on words based on the phonetic similarity of the words fourth and fort, which emphasizes the connection of the linear park with the Kalemegdan fortress, as well as its function in the ecological defense of the city.
